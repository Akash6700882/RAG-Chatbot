# Acme Corp Employee Handbook (Sample)

This is a sample document included for demoing and testing the RAG chatbot's
document ingestion and question-answering pipeline. It is fictional.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off and are reviewed quarterly.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue 18 days of PTO per year, accrued monthly. Unused PTO
up to 5 days may be carried over into the next calendar year. PTO requests must
be submitted at least 5 business days in advance through the HR portal.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses under $75 can be self-approved. Expenses of $75 or more
require manager approval before submission. Reimbursement requests must include
an itemized receipt and be submitted within 30 days of the expense.

## Equipment Policy

New employees receive a laptop and a one-time $300 home-office stipend within
their first 30 days. Equipment must be returned within 14 days of departure.

## Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to treat colleagues, customers, and partners with
respect. Harassment of any kind is prohibited and can be reported confidentially
to HR or through the anonymous ethics hotline.
